About the Conference

The organizing committee of the 2nd. Mexican Artificial Intelligence and Data Science Conference 2026 cordially invite researchers, academics, students, and industry professionals to submit their original, unpublished work. This event is consolidated as a high-level scientific forum designed to discuss recent advances, innovative methodologies, and emerging practical applications in the fields of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science.

Topics of Interest

We seek both theoretical and applied contributions. Topics include, but are not limited to:

  • Software Engineering
  • Natural Language Processing (NLP)
  • Quantum Computing
  • Fuzzy Logic and Neural Networks
  • Human-Computer Interaction (HCI)
  • Automation and Intelligent Systems
  • Computational Cognitive Sciences
  • Image Processing and Computer Vision
  • Data Science & Big Data
  • Simulation and Modeling
  • Neural Networks
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Reinforcement Learning
  • Evolutionary Computing
  • IoT and Smart Cities
  • Parallel Computing applied to Artificial Intelligence
  • Robotics and Mechatronics

Submission Guidelines

Language Requirement: All submissions must be written exclusively in English.
  • Originality: Submitted papers must be original works not currently under review elsewhere.
  • Format: Manuscripts must be formatted following standard guidelines [Plantilla], between 8 and 12 pages long.
  • Review Process: Strictly double-blind. Authors must ensure that the initial PDF submission omits names, affiliations, Github repositories and acknowledgments to guarantee anonymity.
  • Publication & Costs: In a firm commitment to the dissemination of open science, this conference does not charge any Article Processing Charges (APC - No publication fees). Accepted papers will be hosted on Zenodo under a CC BY 4.0 license and assigned a free DOI for Google Scholar indexing.
